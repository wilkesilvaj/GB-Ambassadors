Gracie Barra Ambassadors Pacific Northwest Application

Gracie Barra Pacific Northwest is implementing the GB Ambassadors Program as a platform to united all of our team members in the Pacific Northwest region in support of our top performing athletes. It is our goal to bring all of our schools, instructors, and students together on an effort to help build a great competition team and support individual athletes achieve their full potential in competition. The GB Ambassadors is not a fund raising program. It is rather a team effort designed to build unity within GB in pursuit of excellence in competitive Jiu-Jitsu. Eligibility to receive grants from the GB Ambassadors Pacific Northwest is conditioned to qualifying criteria applicable to the individual athlete and his home school.
